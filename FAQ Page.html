How to order ?

  1. Discover your favorite bouquet, click on the "Buy Flowers" button;
  2. You will then be directed to a direct message on WhatsApp with the seller;
  3. After that, we will provide you with a flower ordering form;
  4. Please fill out the form completely and clearly;
  5. We will send you the bank account number or other payment methods;
  6. Once the product is paid for, we will process the order within a maximum of 24 hours.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept payment through credit or debit cards via BCA, OVO, ShopeePay, and Bank Transfer to accommodate your preferences. For bank transfers, we will process your order after receiving the payment proof. Please provide a screenshot of the payment proof to sekarajengkay@gmail.com along with the order number stated in the subject line (Monday - Sunday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM).
How much the shipping costs?
The shipping fee for orders may vary based on the delivery address. Same-day delivery can be arranged if you place your order before 1:00 PM on the same day. Please note that orders placed after 1:00 PM will be shipped on the following day.
Can i cancel my order ?
You can cancel your order within 24 hours or one (1) day prior to your desired delivery date, and we will process a refund for you. Typically, it takes 3-5 working days to refund the amount back to your account. However, please note that the refund process may take up to 7 working days, depending on the payment method you used when placing the order with Kayla Florist.

This is our cancellation policy that you need to adhere to in order to cancel an order. Failure to comply with the rules we have set may affect your request to cancel the order and receive a refund.
How can I know if my order has been shipped or not?
Certainly, once we have finished packaging and handed it over to our courier, we will provide you with a tracking number so that you can track the package. This will allow you to stay updated on the delivery status of your order.
